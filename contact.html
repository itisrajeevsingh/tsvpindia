Frequently Asked Questions

❓ Who can enroll in TSVP programs?

Youth aged 18–35 from rural/urban underprivileged communities. Specific eligibility (8th/10th pass) varies by course. Government-sponsored seats are free.

❓ Is the training really free?

Yes — for eligible candidates under government schemes like DDU-GKY, PMKVY, and NULM, training is 100% free with stipend support.

❓ Does TSVP guarantee placement?

TSVP provides active placement support through campus-to-corporate drives. DDU-GKY mandates minimum 70% placement, which TSVP consistently achieves.

❓ How do I donate to TSVP?

Via SBI bank transfer (A/C: 33449140444) or UPI (coming soon). Donations are 80G tax deductible and FCRA cleared for international donors.

❓ Can companies partner with TSVP for CSR?

Yes. TSVP is CSR-I registered (SRN: F46910436). Contact us to discuss how CSR funds can be directed to our skill training programs.

❓ Where are TSVP training centers located?

TSVP operates 50+ centers across Bihar (Muzaffarpur, Champaran, Gopalganj, etc.), UP, Jharkhand, Uttarakhand, and Haryana.
