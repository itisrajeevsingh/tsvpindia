    About Tirhut Samagra Vikas Parishad

    Founded in 1992 and registered under the Societies Registration Act 21, 1860 (Reg. No. 533/1992-92), TSVP is a pioneering voluntary organization working across Bihar, Uttar Pradesh, Jharkhand, Uttarakhand, and Haryana.

    Our core mission is to bridge the gap between education and employment for India's rural and urban poor. We believe in making every youth "Eduployable" — not just educated, but genuinely employable with industry-ready skills.
