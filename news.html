World Youth Skills Day — Celebrated at TSVP Centers

New DDU-GKY Batch Inaugurated at Muzaffarpur Center

A new batch of 120+ candidates has been enrolled in DDU-GKY sponsored Healthcare (GDA) and Electrician programs at TSVP's flagship center in Muzaffarpur, Bihar. Training began with orientation and soft skills modules.

Placement Drive — 60 Students Depart from Danapur Railway Station

60 trained youth from TSVP's DDU-GKY program departed for employment from Danapur Railway Station with job offers across Delhi, Haryana, and Gujarat. A proud milestone for both TSVP and the students' families.

GDA Students Visit Maa Janaki Hospital for Clinical Training

Healthcare students in the General Duty Assistant course visited Maa Janaki Hospital at Bairiya Golambar, Muzaffarpur for live clinical exposure — observing patient care, ward management, and medical procedures.

Enrollment for government-sponsored free skill training programs is currently open for youth aged 18–35. Contact TSVP office for eligibility.
