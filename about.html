Our Background & History

Tirhut Samagra Vikas Parishad (TSVP) was founded in 1992 with a singular vision: to uplift the economically weaker sections of society by equipping them with marketable skills and employment opportunities.

Registered under the Societies Registration Act 21, 1860 (Reg. No. 533/1992-92), TSVP began its journey in the Tirhut region of Bihar and has since expanded its operations across Bihar, Uttar Pradesh, Jharkhand, Uttarakhand, and Haryana.

Under the capable leadership of Chief Functionary Baban Kumar Mishra, TSVP has trained over 500,000+ youths, established 50+ training centers, and partnered with multiple government agencies to deliver flagship skill development programs.

TSVP is an ISO 9001:2008 Certified organization, reflecting its commitment to quality, consistency, and continuous improvement in all its training programs.

Our Training Methodology

A structured 4-step approach ensuring effective skill transfer and guaranteed employability.

1

Discuss

Understanding learner needs, local market demands, and industry requirements through structured assessments and consultations.

2

Design

Crafting industry-aligned curriculum with theory modules, practical sessions, soft skills, and on-the-job training components.

3

Develop

Delivering blended learning using classroom instruction, hands-on practice, field visits, and digital learning tools.

4

Deliver

Certified graduates placed in jobs through our wide industry network, placement drives, and campus-to-corporate linkages.
