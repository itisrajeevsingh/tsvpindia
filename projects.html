Central & State Government Schemes

TSVP is an empanelled implementing partner for multiple flagship government skill development programs across India.

🏛️

DDU-GKY — Deen Dayal Upadhyaya Grameen Kaushalya Yojana

MoRD's flagship rural skill development program offering free training and placement to rural poor youth (15–35 years). TSVP operates DDU-GKY centers in Bihar and Uttar Pradesh.

BiharUttar Pradesh
🎓

PMKVY 4.0 — Pradhan Mantri Kaushal Vikas Yojana

National Skill Development Corporation's (NSDC) flagship scheme providing short-term skill training with certification under National Skills Qualification Framework (NSQF).

BiharPan-India
⚡

Kaushal Yuva Program — Bihar Skill Development Mission

Bihar Government's initiative to provide skill training to youth under Bihar Skill Development Mission, focusing on industry-linked trades and self-employment.

Bihar
🏙️

NULM — National Urban Livelihoods Mission

Ministry of Housing & Urban Affairs scheme providing skill training and employment to urban poor. TSVP has implemented NULM projects in Uttarakhand, Jharkhand, and Bihar.

UttarakhandJharkhandBihar
🏭

NIESBUD — Ministry of MSME

National Institute for Entrepreneurship & Small Business Development (NIESBUD) programs for entrepreneurship development under Ministry of Micro, Small & Medium Enterprises.

BiharPan-India
🌆

Bihar Urban Development Agency (NULM)

Urban skill development and livelihood enhancement for economically weaker youth in urban Bihar, implemented through Bihar Urban Development Agency under NULM.

Bihar (Urban)
👩

Women Development Corporation, Govt. of Bihar

Programs empowering women through vocational training in beauty care, fashion designing, and garment making under Bihar Women Development Corporation.

Bihar
📚

BBOSE — HUNAR (Bihar Board of Open Schooling)

Bihar Board of Open Schooling & Examination's HUNAR program providing recognized vocational qualifications to school dropouts and working youth.

Bihar
🌾

Panchayati Raj — Bihar Gram Swaraj Yojna Society

Rural panchayat-level skill and livelihood programs under Bihar Gram Swaraj Yojna Society, reaching the most remote communities across Bihar.

Bihar (Rural)
🏗️

District Rural Development Authority (DRDA)

District-level rural skill programs implemented with DRDAs across East Champaran, West Champaran, Gopalganj, Sitamarhi, and Darbhanga districts.

E. ChamparanW. Champaran GopalganjSitamarhiDarbhanga
⛏️

Central Coal Fields Limited (CSR)

Corporate Social Responsibility funded skill development program with Central Coal Fields Ltd., providing training opportunities to communities around coal mining areas.

Jharkhand
🏥

Manpower Services — Government Hospitals

Skilled healthcare workers placed at Maulana Azad Medical College (Delhi), Chacha Nehru Bal Chikitsalay, Lal Bahadur Shastri Hospital, and Indore Municipal Corporation.

DelhiIndorePan-India
